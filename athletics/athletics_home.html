Topeka Women's Swim Team High Results from 04-27-17 View the results of the swim meet here.
Topeka High won Monday's 12-team Topeka Invitational. The top three teams were very close with Topeka High scoring 42, Derby 41, and Valley Center 40. Defending Centennial League champion Manhattan placed 7th with 24. The Trojan singles players Junior Ian Clifton and Senior Chris Gernon each went 6-0. For the season, Chris Gernon is 17-1 and Ian Clifton is 18-2. Senior Caleb Skrzypek has a winning record at #1 doubles with Senior partners Tanner Ramirez and Jonathan Lee. Sophomore Banks Whitney has won one tournament at #2 doubles with Lee and one tournament with Ramirez. Senior Will Loehr and Sophomore Matt Caudill have also contributed key wins to help the Trojans take first in 5 of our 9 meets. Several others have also rotated between Varsity and JV and contributed to our team record.
